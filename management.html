Joe Calderone

Experience Vice President - Customer Service LIRR August 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months)Long Island Rail Road As Vice President for Customer Service, Marketing & Public Affairs,Market I oversee a LIRR department with a $70 million budget and more than 400 employees responsible for ticket selling, station maintenance, marketing, customer & corporate communications, the Customer Service Center, community and government relations. The LIRR is the nation's busiest commuter rail road, serving more than 83.4 million customers a year. The LIRR operates on 11 branches stretching from the West Side of Manhattan to Montauk at 124 stations and terminals located in virtually every community on Long Island, as in Manhattan, Queens and Brooklyn. The Customer Service area includes ticket selling, ticket technology, station cleaning and the LIRR's emergency Customer Assistance Program. Our Marketing unit partners with Long Island and NYC businesses and attractions to create special deals and getaways that help drive sales of off peak LIRR tickets while supporting local tourism. I also have served as chief spokesman for the LIRR and advise LIRR senior management on media relations and crisis management. During the last six years, we have revamped and enhanced LIRR's customer communications strategy, including creating and expanding our 24-7 Public Information Office, which issues more than 7,000 customer alerts a year and coordinates station announcements as well as updates on electronic station signs, the MTA-LIRR website and with traffic reporting agenices. We also initiated and oversee the LIRR's social media strategy, using Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and the LIRR's own Train Time app for smart phones - all designed to improve customer communications.
